Our Mission

Marian Central Catholic High School is a college preparatory Catholic school. Marian shares with the parents and the parishes of the McHenry Deanery in the teaching mission of the Church. Our school provides students with an education infused with Catholic Christian values designed to promote religious, spiritual, intellectual, physical, social, and emotional development.

Placement Exam

Marian Central Catholic High School will be administering its freshman placement test for the Class of 2018 incoming freshmen on Saturday, January 11, 2014. No appointment is necessary. Students should report to the school at 1001 McHenry Avenue, Woodstock by 8:00 AM on January 11 with the $10 test-processing fee (either in cash or check payable to Marian Central). Pencils will be supplied and students cannot use calculators. Testing begins at 8:15 AM and takes about 3 hours to complete. This is not an entrance exam. We use the results from this test as a tool to properly place students in our academic program so that they have the greatest chance to be successful here at Marian Central Catholic High School.
